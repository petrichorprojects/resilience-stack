## Forward Projection — Where Each Story Goes in 12 Months

**NewEntrant**: Will move from "your analyst" to "your operating team." Expect a 2027 message that bundles agents with workflow execution, not just analysis. Their Series B narrative will pitch "the first agentic retail operating system." This is the L5 territory Tessera vacated.

**LegacyVendor**: Will lean harder on trust, certifications, and procurement-friction reduction. Expect a 2027 message that frames Tessera and NewEntrant as "unproven at enterprise scale." They will not match Tessera's depth — they will commoditize the question of depth.

**InHouse**: Will keep growing under the radar at Fortune 500 retailers with mature data teams. Expect more Snowflake/dbt reference architectures published in 2026 and a community ecosystem that makes "build it yourself" easier each quarter. Win rate against InHouse is the leading indicator nobody currently tracks at Tessera.

**Tessera (no action)**: The "retail operating system" claim is already collapsed. Without rebuild, the narrative drifts to a feature pitch — "deepest retail data, best integrations" — which loses the category conversation entirely.

---

## Where Tessera Leads, Where It Lags

**Leads**:
- **L1 — Origin**: Operator credibility is structurally defensible. Neither NewEntrant nor LegacyVendor can claim it; InHouse doesn't compete on it.
- **L3 — Proof**: 14 of top 50 is real. The crack is freshness, not validity.

**Lags**:
- **L2 — Differentiation**: "Operator-grade depth" is internal language. It does not survive contact with NewEntrant's "your analyst, not your dashboard." A buyer asked to choose between "deeper data" and "an analyst that works" picks the analyst.
- **L4 — Outcome**: $12M is a strong number with a fatal flaw — no published methodology. Procurement teams treat unverified outcome claims as marketing.
- **L5 — Future**: "Retail operating system" was vacated when Gartner reframed the category. Without a forward claim, Tessera reads as the past tense to NewEntrant's future tense.

The two-out-of-five layers Tessera leads are not enough. The three layers Tessera lags are the layers buyers ask about first.
