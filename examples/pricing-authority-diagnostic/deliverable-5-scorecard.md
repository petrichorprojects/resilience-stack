## Top 3 90-Day Actions

1. **Implement quarter-end approval guardrails.** Any deal-close discount above 10% in the last 5 business days of a quarter requires CFO sign-off in writing. No exceptions. Eliminates the 38% quarter-end manager-approved discount class. Owner: **CFO + Head of Sales (Theo + Marcus).** Deadline: **30 days.** Success: zero unapproved >10% quarter-end discounts in Q3 2026, monthly discount % under 14% by day 60.

2. **Pilot tiered provider-band pricing on top-30 customers.** Replace per-provider above 100 with banded pricing (101-200, 201-400, 400+) on top-30 accounts. Measure renewal price and expansion response. Lock for all new deals at day 90. Eliminates the 31% structural discount class. Owner: **Head of Product + CRO (Yuki + Marcus).** Deadline: **60 days.** Success: pilot complete, structural discount class drops from 31% to <8% on new deals, NRR in >100-provider cohort stabilizes.

3. **Reset value metric communication with sales team and customers.** Sales playbook updated with banded pricing narrative. Customer-facing one-pager on the new structure for top-50 prospects. Internal training session for sales on the discount root-cause data — reps see the recoded numbers, not just the policy. Owner: **Head of Marketing + Head of Sales.** Deadline: **14 days.** Success: 100% of sales reps trained, new pricing narrative live in playbook, top-50 prospect outreach complete by day 30.
