## Pricing Architecture Recommendations

- Replace flat per-provider metric with banded tiering at 25 / 100 / 250 providers; preserves SMB simplicity, defends enterprise economics.
- Introduce usage-based overage component on appointments-scheduled-per-month to build a structured expansion pathway (currently 8% of new ARR, target 25%).
- Move legacy / grandfathered accounts to a structured multi-year discount glide-path with documented step-up dates; eliminate open-ended discount commitments at renewal.
