## 0:24 — Segment 2 — The Discount Autopsy

Top 10 discounted deals last quarter pulled up. Rep-claimed reasons: 4x "competitive," 2x "buyer story," 2x "annual commitment," 1x "champion ask," 1x "other." Recoded one by one by walking the deal timeline.

**Facilitator (on Deal #3, 28% discount, rep-coded "competitive")**: "Show me the competing quote in the CRM."

**Dana**: "So 41% of our discount story was wrong."
**Facilitator**: "The story was wrong. The discount happened. That is recoverable. Most of it."

## 1:36 — Segment 6 — Expansion Revenue Gap Calculator + Pricing Architecture Teardown

NRR by quarter: Q4 2024 = 108%, Q1 2025 = 105%, Q2 = 102%, Q3 = 100%, Q4 = 98%, Q1 2026 = 96%. Expansion revenue 2025 = 8% of base (target 25%).
