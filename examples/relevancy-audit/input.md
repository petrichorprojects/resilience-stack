# Worked Example: Relevancy Audit

**Synthetic Co**: B2B vertical SaaS for mid-market legal operations. $30M ARR.

## Context

- Founded 2018. PMF achieved 2021. Plateaued 2024.
- Win rate dropped from 38% (Q2 2024) to 24% (Q1 2026).
- Sales cycle extended from 47 days to 62 days over same window.
- Share of voice flat 18 months despite 2× marketing spend.
- 3 lost deals in last quarter mentioned the same new competitor by name; sales team did not know the competitor existed.

## Top Competitors (prospects compare against)
1. LegacyVendor — incumbent, 15+ years, "trusted/safe"
2. NewEntrant — funded 2024, "AI-native legal ops"
3. InHouse — buyers building their own w/ off-the-shelf tools

## Positioning Statement (verbatim)
"The leading legal operations platform for modern firms."

## Recent Wins
- Mid-Atlantic firm, 80 lawyers — chose us for integrations
- DC boutique, 25 lawyers — chose us for compliance reports
- West Coast firm, 120 lawyers — chose us for partner stickiness

## Recent Losses
- Texas firm, 60 lawyers — went w/ NewEntrant ("they understand AI workflow")
- Florida firm, 90 lawyers — went w/ LegacyVendor ("safer choice")
- NY firm, 110 lawyers — went InHouse ("we'd rather build")

## Evidence Available
- 4 quarters win/loss with reasons coded
- 18 customer surveys with verbatim NPS comments
- 3 competitor positioning statements pulled from their sites
- Analyst quadrant report (Q4 2025)
