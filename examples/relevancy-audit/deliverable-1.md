This statement was last refreshed 2022. It was designed for the 2021-2022 market: post-PMF growth phase, LegacyVendor as the only meaningful competitor, analyst category still labeled "Legal Operations Platforms."

## 3. Competitive Position Drift

Synthetic Co's positioning was designed against LegacyVendor only. The market now has three competitive vectors, and Synthetic Co's positioning addresses none of them directly.

| Vector | LegacyVendor | NewEntrant | InHouse |
|--------|-------------|-----------|---------|
| Their claim | "Trusted by 1,000+ firms since 2009" | "AI-native legal ops, built for the next decade" | (off-the-shelf tools + internal engineering) |
| Buyer reason cited (lost deals) | "Safer choice" (Florida, 90 lawyers) | "They understand AI workflow" (Texas, 60 lawyers) | "We'd rather build" (NY, 110 lawyers) |
| Synthetic Co counter-claim | None explicit. "Modern" implies anti-legacy but never names it. | None. NewEntrant was unknown to sales until the losses landed. | None. The build-vs-buy story is not in any current asset. |

**Finding**: Synthetic Co's positioning was built for a 2-way market (us vs. LegacyVendor). The market is now 4-way (us + LegacyVendor + NewEntrant + InHouse). The positioning has not adapted.

## 5. What's Drifted in the Last 18 Months — Timeline

- **Q3 2024**: Win rate begins drift (38% → 35%). Marketing spend increases. No positioning review.
- **Q4 2024**: NewEntrant raises funding round. Synthetic Co does not log the competitor.
- **Q1 2025**: First "AI workflow" mention in a lost-deal interview. Coded as "fit" reason, not competitive.
- **Q2 2025**: Analyst report drafts circulated; Synthetic Co quoted as "Established Player." No internal response.
- **Q3 2025**: Win rate 28%. Sales cycle 55 days. Marketing doubles paid spend; share of voice unchanged.

- **Q1 2026**: Win rate 24%. Cycle 62 days. CEO commissions Relevancy Audit.

**Pattern**: every signal that fired was misread or unread for 12+ months. Detection lag averages ~9 months. Response lag averages 6+ months. Total decay exposure: ~15 months. The audit lands at the threshold where Stage 3 Displacement becomes the next quarter's risk, not the next year's.
