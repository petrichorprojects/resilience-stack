## Composite Stage Verdict

**Stage 2 Disconnect, borderline Stage 3 Displacement.**

Rationale:
- All 6 Stage 2 indicators are confirmed.
- 1 of 6 Stage 3 indicators (the leading one — buyer language match) is already in Stage 3 territory.
- 1 of 6 Stage 3 indicators (category language emergence) is borderline.
- The lagging Stage 3 indicators (consideration collapse, analyst exclusion) have a 6-9 month detection lag behind the leading ones. They will arrive if the leading indicators are not addressed.

The audit names this configuration "Stage 2, borderline Stage 3" because the leading indicator is already past the line. Treating this as Stage 2 only — and acting on a Stage 2 timeline — is the most common error at this transition. The deliverable assumes a Stage 3-grade urgency on Actions 1 and 2 (Days 1-30) and a Stage 2 timeline on Action 3 (Days 31-60).
