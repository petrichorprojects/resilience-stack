## Summary

**Total load-bearing claims**: 21
**Verified (deck-ready as-stated)**: 9
**Partial / cherry-picked (will fail diligence as currently written)**: 8
**At-risk reference arcs**: 4 of 5 named customers

**The pattern**: The eight partial claims are the ones a sophisticated partner will catch in the first 30 minutes of data-room work. Five of the eight are headline numbers on the cover slide and the "Why-Us" / "Why-This-Trajectory" / "Why-This-Price" slides. The deck does not lose on the operator story or the AI thesis; it loses on the cherry-picking of headline metrics that a trailing recompute will deflate.

**Reference-arc concentration**: Four of five named customers carry diligence risk. The reference list as it stands is the single highest-leverage failure point. Re-anchoring three accounts in 60 days converts the reference arc from net-negative to net-positive. Replace customer D with a different active account; re-anchor customers A and B with new champions before any partner reference call.
